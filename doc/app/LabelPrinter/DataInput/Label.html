    class LabelPrinter::DataInput::Label

    A DataInput::Label is related to an ActiveRecord Label It also implements the Commands::Outputter Each Label is made of formats and drawings which are either barcodes or bitmaps. The formats and drawings are implemented as a DataInput::List
